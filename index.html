I am a PhD student in natural language processing at the Graduate University for Advanced Studies and supervised by Yusuke Miyao at the University of Tokyo. I am interested in how humans create metaphorical expressions and how to model such processes in computational frameworks.

  • Cognitive Linguistics (Metaphor)
